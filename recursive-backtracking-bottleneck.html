The Bottleneck

1
One cell at a time. It can only carve one cell per step — zero parallelism.
2
Dead ends = wasted work. Every red dot is a step that produced nothing. It just retraces its own path.
3
Scales poorly. An n×n grid has n² cells but needs many more than n² total steps because of backtracking.
